Healthy Eating Habits

What is Healthy Eating?

Healthy eating involves consuming a balanced diet with essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, fiber, protein, and healthy fats. It supports immunity, energy levels, and mood regulation.

Tips for Healthy Eating

  • Eat more whole foods, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Stay away from processed foods and sugar-rich items.
  • Drink plenty of water and limit sugary beverages.
  • Eat in portions and avoid late-night snacking.
